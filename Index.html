About Me

Geospatial technology is more than a science to me; it is a language of change, a way of shaping what can happen by revealing the patterns that guide our decisions and transform our communities. Passion is at the center of my journey — whether building scalable GIS systems, automating workflows, or creating interactive platforms, I work with intention to ensure that technology serves people, not the other way around.

Leadership, to me, is about walking alongside others. I thrive on guiding teams through complex challenges, mentoring peers, and empowering organizations to not just adopt technology, but embrace it as a catalyst for growth. I believe the most meaningful role I can play is to inspire confidence in others and create solutions that outlive my individual contribution.

At my core, I am a geospatial innovator, a leader, and a builder of impact. My vision is simple: to align passion with purpose, and to leave behind solutions that tell a story of progress — one project, one organization, and one community at a time.
