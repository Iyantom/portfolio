About Me

At Esri Eastern Africa, I specialize in helping organizations harness the power of GIS to drive smarter decision-making and unlock the full potential of location intelligence. With a strong foundation in Geospatial Engineering and hands-on expertise in Esri technology, I support businesses in deploying, optimizing, and leveraging ArcGIS suite of products to transform spatial data into actionable insights.

Geospatial technology is more than a science to me; it is a language of change, a way of shaping what can happen by revealing the patterns that guide our decisions and transform our communities. Whether building scalable GIS systems, automating workflows, or creating interactive platforms, I work with intention to ensure that technology serves people, not the other way around.

Leadership, to me, is about walking alongside others. I thrive on guiding teams through complex challenges, mentoring peers, and empowering organizations to not just adopt technology, but embrace it as a catalyst for growth. I believe the most meaningful role I can play is to inspire confidence in others and create solutions that outlive my individual contribution.
